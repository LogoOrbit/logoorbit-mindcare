You're Not Alone

Does any of this sound like you?

Whatever you're carrying, it's valid — and it's treatable. Here's how these feelings often show up.

Constant worry or racing thoughts

Your mind won't switch off — sleep, focus, and calm feel out of reach. That's often anxiety, and it responds well to therapy.

Feeling low, empty, or unmotivated

When even small things feel heavy and joy feels far away, that's not laziness — it can be depression, and help works.

Conflict at home or in relationships

Misunderstandings that repeat, distance that grows. Family and couples counseling rebuilds communication and trust.

A child who needs extra support

Speech delays, ADHD, autism, learning differences — early, structured therapy changes a child's whole trajectory.

Not sure which one fits? That's okay — talk to us and we'll figure it out together.

About MindCare

Care that's grounded in science, delivered with heart.

MindCare Services® is a psychotherapy and mental wellness firm based in Karachi. We bring together a multidisciplinary team of specialists — psychologists, physiotherapists, speech therapists, and more — to offer truly holistic care.

Founded by Shaista Tariq, a Psychotherapist, Mental Health Consultant & Educator and proud Member of the Pakistan Psychological Association (PPA), MindCare was built on one belief: no one should have to struggle alone.

"I kept seeing people suffer in silence — unsure if what they felt was 'serious enough' to ask for help. It always is. MindCare exists to make that first step feel less scary."

— Shaista Tariq, Founder & Lead Psychotherapist
